About Us

WE TRANSLATE THE TRENDES INTO COMMERCIAL PRODUCTS

Established in 1990 Al-Riaz Knitwear has become one of the most successful names in making trendy and fashion garments in the Pakistan and our name has a significant presence in Europe. We are working for 24 year but still as days first our priority has always been to produce high quality garment to cater the customer’s basic need of economy and durability.

  • Over 30 years of experience in knitwear production
  • High-quality fabrics and modern garment designs
  • Strong presence in local and European markets
  • Focus on durability, comfort, and customer satisfaction
